My dearest Neha zii,

I have been sitting with these words for a long time, turning them over and over in my heart — not knowing how to begin. So I am just going to say what needs to be said, honestly and fully, without holding anything back.

I am sorry. I am truly, deeply sorry for hurting you. Not in the small way that fades by morning — but in the way that stays. The kind that makes me look at myself and feel ashamed of the moments I was not who you deserved.

You have always given me so much — your patience, your warmth, your laughter, your love. And there were moments when I took all of that for granted. I did things that made you feel less than you are, and I hate myself for that. You never deserved any of it.

You are not just someone I love, Neha zii. You are the person who makes ordinary days feel like something worth remembering. The way you smile, the way you care, the way you still show up even when things are hard — it means more to me than I have ever managed to say properly.

I am not making excuses. There are none good enough. I am simply asking — gently, with everything I have — for your forgiveness. Not because I deserve it, but because I am committed to being better. For you. For us.

I am working on myself every single day. And I need you to know — no matter what — my love for you is not just a feeling that comes and goes. It is a decision I make every morning, every evening, and every quiet moment in between.

You mean the world to me. You always have. You always will.

Forever yours, Sudhanshu
